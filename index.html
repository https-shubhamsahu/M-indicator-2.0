Born out of
commuter frustration

Launched in 2010 by Sachin Suryakant Teke, m-Indicator started because one tech-commuter was fed up with chaotic train information. It wasn't just built for tech; it was built as a digital lifeline to navigate complex local transport realistically.

A Mobility Data Goldmine

With tens of millions of daily routes queried and shared, m-Indicator holds one of India's richest mobility datasets. This positions it perfectly to spearhead future urban traffic routing and expansive analytic capabilities.

About Mobond

Mobond launched our first free product m-Indicator in May 2010. Mobond is a combination of two words 'Mob' and 'Bond'. The name suggests our aim is 'bonding the mob' or 'bonding the angry crowd'.

Mobond is dedicated to building software products which will be fundamentally useful to the masses. We regularly collect and process massive amounts of transit data from cities and towns in India to provide updated, reliable travel information to millions of daily commuters.
